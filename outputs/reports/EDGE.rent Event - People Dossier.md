## 🎯 CHIEF'S STRATEGIC READ (read this first)

**The room is the product.** EDGE's pitch is "we activate built-in revenue streams." Look at who's in the room and you see *what those streams actually are* — a **vendor marketplace with revenue share**, not magic software:
- **Ryse (Tom Smith)** — rent-advance fintech that pays the PM **2% on every advance**. That's a literal "new revenue stream."
- **Resident Inspect (Aaron Cooper)** — per-inspection fees (~$45–65) you outsource/mark up.
- **VirtuallyinCredible (Logan Breen)** — offshore/virtual staffing you bill through.
- **Consultants (Deb Newell, Marco Montes)** — the **credibility halo**. Respected NARPM names that make the pitch feel like an industry consensus, not a sales event.
- **Operator partner (Blakely Hughes)** — the "peer testimonial." Already bought in; took the first market outside Tampa.

**Implication for your decision:** Every person in that room profits if you say yes. The "revenue guarantee" is largely *"join our vendor ecosystem and earn referral/markup income."* That's real money — but it's a **channel/affiliate model**, not proprietary software value. Ask which revenue streams are EDGE IP vs. just third-party vendor referrals you could sign up for independently (without EDGE's subscription or lock-in).

**Engineering-depth signal:** EDGE's lead engineer (Sri Kurapati) self-describes as **~5+ years full-stack at Rent Solutions** (LinkedIn), vs. the "12+ years" in the event bio. If EDGE is ~one founder-operator + a small in-house dev team + a vendor marketplace, "they're years ahead and I can't match their pace" gets a lot weaker. **Probe the real size of the engineering team (Question for Sri).**

**AppFolio vs Rentvine tie-in:** Marco Montes / Reconcile Daily does **AppFolio-only** trust reconciliation — the exact F2 feature in your own blueprint. Worth a sidebar with him on where AppFolio trust-recon actually breaks at scale.

**Data flags:** Deb Newell's bio claims she founded a "PMX platform" — **could not be verified anywhere**; may be inaccurate. Sri's tenure conflicts (5+ vs 12+). Verify in person.
