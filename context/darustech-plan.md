# DarusTech Inc — Business Plan Summary

Full plan: Obsidian vault → RentorOS/DarusTech Inc - Business Plan.md

## The Company
AI automation agency specializing in property management technology. Owns all Rentor software. Rentor pays subscription. Then sells "Rentor Workspace" to other PM companies.

## Business Model
- Rentor Workspace subscriptions: $500-10,000/mo per PM company
- AI automation consulting: project-based ($5K-50K) for non-PM clients
- Rentor Direct add-ons: per-transaction fees for DIY landlords

## Revenue Targets
- 2026: $50K (consulting + first subs)
- 2027: $500K-1M (20-50 customers)
- 2028: $5-10M (100+ customers)

## Go-to-Market
Phase 1: Prove on Rentor → Phase 2: NARPM launch → Phase 3: Beta customers → Phase 4: Scale

## Not Yet Incorporated
Timing: after NARPM validates demand (May 2026)
