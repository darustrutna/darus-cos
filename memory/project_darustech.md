**DarusTech** — AI automation agency Darus will start.

**Structure:**
- DarusTech owns all software being developed (Rentor Scale, MyRentor, Inbox, Ledger, Direct, etc.)
- Rentor (the PM company) pays DarusTech a subscription to use the tools
- Rentor is the test run / first customer — proves the model works on a real PM company
- DarusTech then offers "Rentor Workspace" to other property management companies
- DarusTech also accepts other clients selectively (e.g., Hanford Community Garden RSVP system)

**The vision:** Google Workspace, but for property management. A suite of integrated tools (CRM, owner portal, inbox, accounting, direct listings) that any PM company can subscribe to.

**Why it works:**
- Every tool is battle-tested on Rentor's 1,137+ doors before going to market
- Darus understands PM operations deeply — building from operator perspective
- The Appfolio integration makes it sticky — connects to industry-standard PM software
- Revenue from software subscriptions compounds on top of PM revenue

**Current state (April 2026):** DarusTech not yet incorporated. All software being built inside the Rentor monorepo. The quiet launch at NARPM (April 28) is the first market test. Business plan drafted at `RentorOS/DarusTech Inc - Business Plan.md`.

**How to apply:** When making architectural decisions on Rentor tools, consider multi-tenancy and white-labeling early. But don't over-engineer — Rentor is customer #1 and the priority is shipping, not building a perfect SaaS platform.
