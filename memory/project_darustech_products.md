- **Voice/AI:** Rentor Voice (Grok) — AI outbound calls, "One Team, One Voice"
- **Workflow:** Rentor Process — automation engine (LeadSimple-inspired)

- **External:** MyRentor.com (client portal) + Rentor Direct (DIY landlords)
- **Signatures:** Rentor Sign (BoloSign API)
- **Integrations:** Rentor Appfolio (Chrome extension)
