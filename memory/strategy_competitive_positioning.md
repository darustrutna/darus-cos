## The Existential Objection

**"Why bet on a startup when [Appfolio | Buildium | RentVine] is adding AI features eventually?"**

This is the #1 objection from PM operators with established PMS contracts. Surfaced first by Gray Hall (Evernest, 30K doors). Will appear from every Appfolio customer.

## The Answer — Memorize This

**Greenfield vs. Retrofit.**

> "Appfolio is bolting AI onto a 20-year-old property management system. Rentor Workspace is built AI-native from the foundation up. They're retrofitting. We're greenfield. By the time their AI feature ships, we'll be three product cycles ahead, deeply integrated with the operator's workflow, and learning faster from every customer interaction."

## Why This Works

1. **Frames the comparison correctly:** Stops being "feature for feature" — becomes "architecture for architecture."
2. **Captures the moat:** Every interaction trains Rentor's system. Appfolio's bolt-ons don't have that loop.
3. **Reframes the risk:** Betting on the startup = betting on the right architecture. Betting on Appfolio = betting that retrofit catches up. Operators understand retrofit always loses to greenfield in tech.
4. **Time pressure:** "Three product cycles ahead by the time they ship" creates urgency to start now.

> "Every call your team makes goes through Rentor Voice. Every transcript trains our coaching model. Every coaching insight that closes a deal makes the next call smarter. Six months in, you're not just using AI — you're running a private AI that learns YOUR business. No competitor can copy that loop, because the data is yours."
